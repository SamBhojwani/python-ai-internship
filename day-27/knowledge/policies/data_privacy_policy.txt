The company collects and processes personal data only for legitimate business purposes and in compliance with applicable data protection laws. Employee and customer data must be stored securely and access limited to those who need it for their role. Data retention periods are defined per data category, with personal data deleted once no longer needed unless legally required to retain it. Any data breach must be reported to the data protection officer within 24 hours of discovery. Third party vendors handling company data must sign a data processing agreement before access is granted.
