Employees may claim reimbursement for business related expenses such as travel, client meals and approved software subscriptions. All expense claims must be submitted through the finance portal with original receipts attached within 30 days of the expense. Expenses above 5000 rupees require prior approval from the reporting manager before being incurred. Personal expenses, alcohol and entertainment unrelated to business purposes are not reimbursable. Reimbursements are typically processed within 10 working days of an approved claim.
