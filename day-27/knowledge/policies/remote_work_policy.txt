Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval and role requirements. Remote employees are expected to be available during core working hours of 10am to 4pm for meetings and collaboration. A stable internet connection and a quiet working environment are required for remote work days. Company equipment provided for remote work remains the property of the company and must be returned upon termination of employment. Remote work arrangements are reviewed periodically and may be adjusted based on business needs.
