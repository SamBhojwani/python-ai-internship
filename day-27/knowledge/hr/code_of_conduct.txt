Employees are expected to treat colleagues, clients and partners with respect and professionalism at all times. Discrimination, harassment or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated and should be reported to HR immediately. Employees must avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any outside business activities that may compete with company interests. Confidential company information must not be shared with unauthorized parties, including on social media. Violations of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
