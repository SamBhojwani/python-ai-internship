Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, accrued monthly. Sick leave of up to 10 days per year is available with a medical certificate required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days. Leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 3 working days in advance for planned leave. Unused annual leave can be carried forward up to a maximum of 5 days into the next calendar year, after which it is forfeited. Maternity leave is 26 weeks and paternity leave is 2 weeks, both fully paid.
