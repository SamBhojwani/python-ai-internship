New employees should complete their laptop and account setup on day one with support from the IT team. HR will schedule an orientation session covering company policies, benefits enrollment and team introductions within the first week. New hires are assigned a buddy from their team to help them get familiar with tools and processes. All mandatory compliance trainings, including data privacy and workplace conduct, must be completed within the first 30 days. A 90 day probation review will be conducted with the reporting manager to assess onboarding progress.
