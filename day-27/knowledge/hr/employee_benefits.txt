All full-time employees are eligible for health insurance coverage starting from day one of employment, covering the employee and up to 4 dependents. The company provides a annual wellness allowance of 10000 rupees for gym memberships or wellness programs. Employees are enrolled in a provident fund scheme with matching employer contributions. A meal allowance is provided for employees working from office. Annual performance bonuses are paid based on individual and company performance, typically disbursed in April.
