Q: How do I apply for leave?
A: Leave requests can be submitted through the HR portal at least 3 working days in advance for planned leave.

Q: When will I receive my salary?
A: Salaries are credited on the last working day of each month.

Q: How do I update my bank details for payroll?
A: Update your bank details through the HR portal under the payroll information section.

Q: Who do I contact for benefits related questions?
A: Reach out to the HR benefits team through the HR portal or email hrbenefits@company.com.
