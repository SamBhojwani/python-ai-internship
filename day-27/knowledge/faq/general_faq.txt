Q: How do I reset my company account password?
A: Go to the IT self-service portal and click Forgot Password. A reset link will be sent to your registered email.

Q: Who do I contact for IT issues?
A: Raise a ticket through the IT helpdesk portal or email itsupport@company.com for urgent issues.

Q: How do I book a meeting room?
A: Meeting rooms can be booked through the internal calendar system by selecting an available room and time slot.

Q: What is the process for requesting new software?
A: Submit a software request through the IT portal with business justification, which will be reviewed by your manager and IT.
